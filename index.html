About me



My name in Chinese is 张宇.

I'm currently a postdoc researcher in Math Department at Nankai University. My collaborating advisor is Xiangjun Wang.

My primary research is in the area of algebraic topology, more specifically in homotopy theory.

More detailed information about me can be found in my CV.

    • Research



    I am interested in model categories/infinity categories, localization/completion methods and structured ring spectra. On the computational side, I am also interested in various spectral sequence computations towards the stable homotopy groups of spheres. Recently, I am also exploring the area of motivic homotopy theory.

    Teaching

    I am passionate about teaching and enjoy getting to know my students. If you are a current (or former) student looking to get in touch, please do not hesitate to e-mail me or drop by my office.

    Generally, I post any and all course-related materials (lecture notes, quiz solutions, extra problems, etc.) to our course's Carmen page, rather than here on my personal webpage. If you are a student looking for these resources, please check Carmen.

    In addition to teaching courses, I have served as a teaching mentor both in our department's summer TA training course, as well as to other TA's during their first semester of teaching.
